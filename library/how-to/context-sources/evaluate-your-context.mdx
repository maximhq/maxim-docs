title: Evaluate your context
description: Learn how to evaluate the quality and effectiveness of your RAG context sources in Maxim for improved AI performance and accuracy.

Context sources in Maxim allow you to expose your RAG pipeline via a simple endpoint irrespective of the complex steps within it. This context source can then be linked as a variable in your prompt or workflow and selected for evaluation [here](/evaluate/how-to/evaluate-prompts/rag-quality).
